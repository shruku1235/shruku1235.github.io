Iced Strawberry Matcha

*can make hot too :) *

Ingredients:

  1. 1 scoop matcha powder
  2. 8 oz strawberry milk
  3. 0-3 pumps of vanilla syrup
  4. your desired amount of iced cubes
  5. 4 oz hot water
Pump vanilla syrup into your cup if desired -->
Use a milk frother or whisk for 30 seconds to mix the matcha powder and hot water -->
Fill your cup with your desired amount of ice -->
Pour strawberry milk into the cup (steam if you want it hot)-->
Add your matcha mixture to the cup -->
Add any extra toppings (like freeze-dried strawberries) if you want and enjoy!
