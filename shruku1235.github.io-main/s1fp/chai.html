Masala Chai

*can make iced too :) *

Ingredients:

  1. 2 tsps masala chai leaves
  2. 10 oz milk of your choice
  3. your desired amount of honey
  4. your desired amount of iced cubes
  5. your desired spices
Boil 6oz of water on the stove -->
Once the water is boiling, add your masala chai leaves and any other spices you want to add to the pot -->
Add honey to the pot and let the mixture boil -->
Add milk to the pot and let it boil for 30 more seconds (unless you want it iced) -->
*If iced, add ice cubes to your cup and pour cold milk into it* -->
Strain the tea into your cup and enjoy!
